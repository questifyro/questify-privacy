1. Introduction

Questify is a mobile application designed for urban exploration and daily interactive quests. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with GDPR.

2. Data We Collect

  • Email address
  • User ID
  • Selected city
  • Quest progress and points
  • Photos uploaded for quest validation

We do NOT collect sensitive personal data such as face photos, ID documents, or payment card details.

3. How We Use Data

  • Assign daily quests
  • Validate completed quests
  • Calculate points and rankings
  • Improve app experience

4. Sponsored Quests

Sponsored quests may be available in the app. Brands do not receive personal user data. Only anonymous statistics are shared.

5. Payments

Payments are handled by official platforms (e.g. App Store). Questify does not store card data.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or delete your data. Contact us at contact@questifyro.com for any request.

7. Children

Questify is not intended for users under 13 years old.

8. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be published on this page.
